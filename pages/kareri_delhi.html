Delhi - Kareri - Delhi
About Trip

Get off the beaten track with the majestic Kareri Lake Trek and explore the divine nature of the Himalayas. Located high between the Dhauladhar range, Kareri Lake is a shallow fresh water lake. This thrilling trail takes you through lush green forests and alpine grasslands to a world of scenic beauty. This 3 days trek covers approximately 17 kms. of distance (one side) and serves as a base for trekking further into the Dhauladhar.


Feast your eyes with the beauty of the Kareri Lake and make the most of your holidays at Kareri.

  • Duration – 2 Nights/ 3 Days
  • Trek Height & Distance – 2934 m and 17 kms. (one side)
  • Starting Point – Delhi Kareri Delhi
  • Transportation – Transportation included Delhi to Delhi
  • Price - 5,999 /- Per Person
