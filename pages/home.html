Overview

Himachal Kareri Lake is not a company but a group of experienced and well trained trekkers who are knwon as Ninja's of the locals. We all established Trekker's Stop as to follow our passion and are now continously growing across india and are organising trek at various key destinations like Trihund Chamba Valley Bir Village: The heart of Himachal and may more places across India.
We also organise paragliding and bonfire camps at trek as per trekkers choice.
