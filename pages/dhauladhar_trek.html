Dhauladhar Trek

About Trip

A high altitude town with colonial touch will bring you closer to the aura of the Dalhousie. The vast green patches and lined up by lush green coniferous brings a soothing smile on face and calmness in mind. The tour will take you in and around Dalhousie. Explore the markets, one realizes how this hill station is built on and around the five hills.


Thick forests of pine and deodar covered the hill slopes with the trunks of the trees easily a few feet wide.

  • Duration – 5 Nights/ 6 Days
  • Trek Height & Distance – 45-50 K.Ms Approx
  • Starting Point – Dharamshala Dhauladhar Range Bhagsu Gaon
  • Transportation – Transportation included Dsharamshala to Dharamshala
  • Price - 5,999 /- Per Person
    • Journey Overview
    Day 1
    • Pickup from Dharamshala to Kareri Village with breakfast on go
    • Stoppage to explore the eyes catching views
    • Reach to Kareri Village and set out to trek for Kareri Lake
    • With having break on Reoti base, our destination will be to reach Lake by evening
    • Bonfire and Camping near lake with party and dinner( Veg)
    Day 2
    • Leaving the Lake view by morning, our next desination will be Naghra Base Camp
    • Trek down 9 Km to reach, with break at minkaini
    • with heavy heart to miss that mesmerising view from top, set out to meadows for trek
    • Reach to Naghra base by evening and adore the unforgettable view of sunset from 1st sister lake
    • Ending day with view, enjoy the night with camping and bonfire with dinner.
    Day 3
    • Starting day with breakfast, set to Naag Dal trek (12KM trek)
    • With passing through deep woods and mother nature to reach lam dal lake for lunch
    • With resting aside of a natural source of mineral water with clear view of fish in water
    • Reach to Naagdal Lake and camping on river side with bonfire and Veg Dinner
    Day 4
    • After enjoying the night, we set out to most awaited trek to Laka
    • After travelling half of the distance of 6KM, we reach the top of Indrahara Top
    • Enjoy the lunch in the deep forest behind, the thrill of experiencing Himalayan Range
    • Set Back to trek to Laka and aim to reach destinationby evening for snacks and exploring woods and areas while in camping.
    Day 5
    • The most awaited trek is here, Thrihund trek (10KM)
    • After having breakfast, set to the path to valleys and grassangs and woods to reach trihund
    • With aim to reach there by evening, explore the local area and small treks to nearby areas to explore views
    • Setting the trip to end soon, last night camp at trihund and bonfire with party night and dinner.
    Day 6
    • Now as trips end, the final trek start to Bhagsu town
    • After reaching there, resting for a while and exploring the market and shops our trip ends
    • Drop to Dharamshala/Bhagsu town with good memories
