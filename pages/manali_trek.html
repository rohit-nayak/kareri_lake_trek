Manali Trek

About Trip

Since times long past, Manali for a couples destination in India has been very popular. Featuring at the top of the list of honeymoon destinations for newly married couples, a Manali couple package offers an amalgamation of leisure, adventure, spirituality, and fun. This destination is such that you will be enamoured by the breath-taking views of the surrounding mountains, the gushing of the Beas River as it flows through the town, and the lush expansive forests. A Manali couple package offers you the perfect setting to create the most romantic memories that will last you for the rest of your lives.


  • Duration – 6 Nights/ 7 Days
  • Starting Point – Delhi Manali Delhi
  • Transportation – Transportation included Delhi to Delhi
  • Price - 5,999 /- Per Person
    • Journey Overview
    Day 1
    • Reached Dharamshala from Delhi via Vovlo
    • Travel to McLeod Ganj via markets and stoppages
    • Checkin to Hotel and Night Stay with Foods
    Day 2
    • Trek Starts to Kareri Village (12 K.M Trek)
    • Trekking to various up and down reaching to lunch destination at Ghera Gaon
    • Exploring Village life and some rest then leave for remaining trek
    • Reach to Kareri Village by evening and stay at village enjoying the feel of nature.
    Day 3
    • Trek Starts from Village to Kareri Lake (13 K.M Trek)
    • Trekking to various splines reaching to Reoti Base for Lunch
    • Reaching To Lake by evening and enjoying Camping and Bonfire evening near Kareri Lake
    • Whole Night Party (as per your own convinence)
    Day 4
    • Travel Back to Kareri Lake to Village
    • Lunch at Kareri Village and set back to base at Dharamshala
    • Enjoying the market and local life at Dharamshala
    • Set Back to Delhi with Lots of Memories and mesmerising views in heart.
